Green web hosting is a greener, more environmentally friendly way to host websites. Most green web hosting companies aim to provide high-quality services while doing everything they can to limit their carbon emissions.

There are so many companies as shown in the visualization who are actively commiting to providing greener services. Why does this matter? According to Mckinsey, technology is responsible for 1 percent of greenhouse gas emissions, as of now. It will only skyrocket as technology grows with AI, supercomputing, etc. as those technologies require a lot of energy.
